Drunken Brawl Ends in Fatal Chennai Murder

2025-03-22T18:23:00+00:00

In Chennai, a 19-year-old man named Jegan was arrested for the murder of 19-year-old college student Lokesh. Lokesh's body was found in an empty plot in Thiruvalangadu police limits two days after he was last seen visiting his grandmother.
Police investigations revealed that Jegan and Lokesh had a drunken brawl, which resulted in Jegan killing Lokesh.
