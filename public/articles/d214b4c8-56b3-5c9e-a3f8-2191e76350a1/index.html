SpiceJet Soars: New Routes and Destinations

2025-03-27T03:27:00+00:00

SpiceJet is launching new domestic flights, starting March 30th, to expand its network.
The airline will connect Pune to Varanasi and Chennai with direct flights. The Pune-Varanasi route will operate daily, departing from Pune at 12:10 pm and arriving in Varanasi at 2:30 pm. The return flight departs from Varanasi at 3 pm and arrives in Pune at 4:55 pm. The Pune-Chennai route will also operate daily, departing from Pune at 2:05 am and arriving in Chennai at 4:25 am.
Other new routes include Ahmedabad to Srinagar, Bengaluru to Goa, and three new destinations: Tuticorin, Porbandar, and Dehradun. SpiceJet will also launch a twice-weekly (Tuesdays and Thursdays) non-stop flight connecting Porbandar to Mumbai starting April 1st, 2025.
